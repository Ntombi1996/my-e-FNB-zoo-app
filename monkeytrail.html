Monkey Trail

On a Monkey Train you experience a short hike which will give you an opportunity to spot monkeys along the way and get to also face fairly challenging climbs with uneven steps. You will get to experience our cable ride where you get to see the perfect view of the Pine city Zoo.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

The Monkey Trail is at the eastern side of the zoo, next to the Insect House.
